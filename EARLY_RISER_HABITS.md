# Early Riser - 5 Sample Habits

## Identity: Early Riser

### 1. Morning Hydration
- **Habit**: Drink a glass of water
- **Time**: 05:30
- **Location**: Bedroom
- **Stack After**: Wake up
- **Implementation**: After I wake up at 05:30 in the bedroom, I will drink a glass of water
- **Make it Obvious**: Place water bottle on nightstand before bed
- **Make it Attractive**: Use a beautiful glass bottle, add lemon slice
- **Make it Easy**: Fill bottle the night before
- **Make it Satisfying**: Feel refreshed and energized immediately
- **Schedule**: Daily (Mon-Sun)

### 2. Morning Stretch
- **Habit**: Do 5-minute stretching routine
- **Time**: 05:35
- **Location**: Bedroom
- **Stack After**: Drink water
- **Implementation**: After I drink water at 05:35 in the bedroom, I will do 5-minute stretching routine
- **Make it Obvious**: Keep yoga mat rolled out beside bed
- **Make it Attractive**: Play calming morning music
- **Make it Easy**: Just do 3 stretches to start
- **Make it Satisfying**: Feel body wake up and energized
- **Schedule**: Daily (Mon-Sun)

### 3. Morning Meditation
- **Habit**: Meditate for 10 minutes
- **Time**: 05:45
- **Location**: Living room
- **Stack After**: Stretch
- **Implementation**: After I stretch at 05:45 in the living room, I will meditate for 10 minutes
- **Make it Obvious**: Set up meditation cushion in corner
- **Make it Attractive**: Light a candle, use calming app
- **Make it Easy**: Start with just 2 minutes of breathing
- **Make it Satisfying**: Track streak on calendar, feel centered
- **Schedule**: Daily (Mon-Sun)

### 4. Morning Planning
- **Habit**: Review and plan my day
- **Time**: 06:00
- **Location**: Kitchen
- **Stack After**: Meditate
- **Implementation**: After I meditate at 06:00 in the kitchen, I will review and plan my day
- **Make it Obvious**: Keep planner on kitchen table
- **Make it Attractive**: Use colorful pens, enjoy morning coffee while planning
- **Make it Easy**: Just write top 3 priorities
- **Make it Satisfying**: Feel organized and in control
- **Schedule**: Daily (Mon-Sun)

### 5. Morning Reading
- **Habit**: Read for 15 minutes
- **Time**: 06:15
- **Location**: Living room
- **Stack After**: Plan my day
- **Implementation**: After I plan my day at 06:15 in the living room, I will read for 15 minutes
- **Make it Obvious**: Place book on favorite chair
- **Make it Attractive**: Create cozy reading nook with good lighting
- **Make it Easy**: Just read one page to start
- **Make it Satisfying**: Track pages read, feel accomplished before others wake
- **Schedule**: Daily (Mon-Sun)

---

## Benefits of Being an Early Riser:
- ✅ Quiet, uninterrupted time for yourself
- ✅ Sense of accomplishment before the day begins
- ✅ Better mental clarity and focus
- ✅ Consistent morning routine builds discipline
- ✅ More productive throughout the day
