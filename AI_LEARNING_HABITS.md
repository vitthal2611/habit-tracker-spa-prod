# AI Learning - Sample Habits

## Identity: AI Learner

### 1. Daily AI Reading
- **Habit**: Read AI/ML articles for 15 minutes
- **Time**: 07:00
- **Location**: Home office
- **Stack After**: Have morning coffee
- **Implementation**: After I have morning coffee at 07:00 in the home office, I will read AI/ML articles for 15 minutes
- **Make it Obvious**: Bookmark AI newsletters (The Batch, Import AI), keep tablet on desk
- **Make it Attractive**: Follow interesting AI researchers on Twitter, save exciting papers
- **Make it Easy**: Just read headlines and summaries to start
- **Make it Satisfying**: Highlight key insights, share learnings with community
- **Schedule**: Daily (Mon-Sun)
